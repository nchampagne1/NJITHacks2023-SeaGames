Oceana

Oceana focuses on protecting and restoring the world's oceans through policy advocacy, scientific research,

and public awareness campaigns.They work to combat overfishing, protect marine habitats, and reduce pollution.

Marine Conservation Institute

This organization is dedicated to protecting and conserving the world's oceans and marine life.

They focus on creating marine protected areas and sustainable fisheries management.

Surfrider Foundation

Surfrider is primarily focused on the protection of coastal and ocean environments.

They work on issues such as water quality, plastic pollution, and beach access.

Project AWARE

This non-profit organization is dedicated to the protection of underwater environments and marine life.

They focus on issues such as marine debris and shark conservation.

Coral Restoration Foundation

This organization is dedicated to the preservation and restoration of coral reefs, which are critical to marine ecosystems.

They grow and transplant corals to help rebuild damaged reefs.

Sea Shepherd Conservation Society

Sea Shepherd is known for its direct action campaigns to protect marine life.

They engage in activities like anti-whaling and anti-poaching operations to defend marine species.
